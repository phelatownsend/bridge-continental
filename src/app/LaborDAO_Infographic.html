The LaborDAO

A Revolving Growth Fund for the 21st Century Labor Movement

The Challenge & The Opportunity

The Momentum

Momentum is building on two fronts: Independent workers are self-organizing in tech and services, while traditional unions are seeing major growth (e.g., Teamsters 856 +8,000 members).

The Barrier: Cost

Organizing is incredibly expensive. Traditional funds are strained and cannot finance every opportunity, leaving this new momentum on the table.

The LaborDAO Growth Flywheel

Our model funds organizing campaigns and replenishes itself upon success, creating an accelerating, self-sustaining growth fund.

Capital Source: The 80/15/5 Engine

Anyone can contribute to the endowment. The 80% is the user's claim. The 20% "donation" is split between the perpetual endowment (15%) and DAO operations (5%).

Sustainable Operations Plan

The 5% operations fee funds the DAO's long-term health, including development, legal, and security audits.

Note: These percentages are subject to change depending on needs.

Projected Loan Deployment

Projections are based on 5% APY on the 15% endowment, *plus* principal repayments from prior loans, creating accelerating growth.
